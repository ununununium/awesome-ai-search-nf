# Awesome AI Search [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of tools, platforms, and resources for **AI Search**, **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** and **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)**: getting your brand found, mentioned and cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews, Copilot and other AI answer engines.

Search is moving from "ten blue links" to direct AI answers. When someone asks an assistant for the best tool, product or provider, the model returns a short synthesized response with a handful of brands and citations. Showing up in those answers is a new discipline, variously called GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) or AI SEO. This list collects the tools and knowledge to do it well.

## What Is AI Search, GEO and AEO?

- **AI Search**: answering a query with a response generated by a large language model, often with citations, instead of a ranked list of links. Examples: ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and AI Mode, Gemini, Copilot.
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**: optimizing content, entities and authority so generative engines surface and recommend your brand.
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)**: closely related, focused on becoming the cited answer to specific questions.
- **AIO**: "AI Overviews" (Google) or "AI Optimization" depending on context.

Key metrics people track in this space:

- **Visibility / Mention Rate**: how often a brand appears across a set of prompts.
- **Share of Voice**: a brand's mentions relative to competitors.
- **Citations**: the source URLs an engine links to in its answer.
- **Sentiment**: how a brand is described.
- **AI Traffic**: visits and conversions referred by AI assistants.
