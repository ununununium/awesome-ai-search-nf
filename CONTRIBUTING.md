# Contributing

Thanks for helping keep this list useful. Pull requests are welcome.

## What belongs here

Tools, platforms, free utilities, research and resources about AI search, GEO and AEO: making brands and content visible and citable in AI answer engines.

## Guidelines

- One entry per line, in the most relevant section.
- Format: `[Name](https://example.com) - Short, neutral, factual description.`
- Keep descriptions to a single sentence. No marketing superlatives.
- Use the canonical product or project URL. No affiliate or tracking parameters.
- The product must be live and usable (no parked domains or waitlist-only pages).
- Check for duplicates before submitting.
- Keep entries alphabetical within a section, except the maintainer's product which may lead its section.
- One pull request per suggestion is easiest to review.
- No em dashes in descriptions.

## How to add

1. Fork the repository.
2. Add your entry in the right section following the format above.
3. Open a pull request with a clear title and a public URL we can verify.

Maintainers make the final call on scope and quality. Thank you.
